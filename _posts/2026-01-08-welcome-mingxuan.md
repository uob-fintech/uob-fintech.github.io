A warm welcome to Dr Mingxuan Yi, who has joined University of Bristol as Senior Research Associate in AI for Finance. Mingxuan has a PhD in Statistical Theory of Machine Learning from School of Mathematics, University of Bristol, with thesis title [Discrepancy Minimizations: From Sample Space to Parameter Space](https://research-information.bris.ac.uk/ws/portalfiles/portal/437968196/Divergence_Minimizations_revised_1_.pdf). He also has MSc in Statistics and Operational Research (University of Edinburgh) and BSc in Mathematics and Physics (Fuzhou University). Prior to joining us, he worked as Associate Machine Learning Scientist at J.P. Morgan, London. Mingxuan will be working with Prof John Cartlidge on collective intelligence for stability in financial markets, as part of EPSRC's AI Hub [AI4CI](https://ai4ci.ac.uk/).
