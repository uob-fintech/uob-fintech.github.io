A warm welcome to Dr Yang Yang, who has joined University of Bristol as Lecturer in Financial Technology. Yang has a PhD in Mechanical Engineering, University of Birmingham, UK, on [The effect of nanoforces on the attachment of cells](https://etheses.bham.ac.uk//id/eprint/8502/). Most recently, he worked as Postdoctoral Research Associate in Finance at Tsinghua University, China, with Prof Tang Ke. Yang's research interests include blockchain, cryptocurrencies, and decentralised finance.
