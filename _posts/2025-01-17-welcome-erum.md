A warm welcome to Ms Erum Iftikhar, who is visiting us for a 6-month PhD visit, supervised by Dr Wei Wei and Prof Cartlidge. Erum is a second-year PhD student at Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore, Milan, where she is funded by a PNNR Scholarship and supervised by Prof Elena Beccalli. While visiting Bristol, Erum will be exploring risk in de-fi lending mechanisms.
