A warm welcome to Dr Wei Wei, who has joined University of Bristol as Lecturer in Financial Technology. Wei has recently received a PhD in Mathematical Finance from University of Sussex Business School, UK, under the supervision of Prof Carol Alexander. Wei also holds an MSc in Economics and a BSc in Information and Computing Science. Her research interests include simulation, high-dimensional systems, and machine learning applications in finance.
