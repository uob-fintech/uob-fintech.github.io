I am pleased to share that Dr. Zixing Song has been selected to deliver a featured talk on graph machine learning at the [New Faculty Highlights program](https://aaai.org/conference/aaai/aaai-26/nfh-call/), 40th AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI-26) in Singapore. This highly competitive program at one of the world's leading AI conference venues showcases rising stars in the artificial intelligence community who have recently started their faculty careers. This selection is a recognition of the impact and quality of Zixing's research agenda. Congratulations, Zixing!
