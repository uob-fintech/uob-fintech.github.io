A warm welcome to Mr Theodoros Constantinides, who begins his PhD studies this month, supervised by Dr John Cartlidge and Dr Yan Ge. Theo is well-known to the group. He spent the last year working with us as Hybrid TA, helping to deliver our MSc Financial Technology with Data Science. Previously, Theo graduated from University of Bristol, with First Class MEng in Computer Science. Theo has been awarded a Computer Science Doctoral Training Associate (DTA) Studentship to support his studies for a Computer Science PhD. As DTA, Theo will work on his PhD for 2 years part-time, while also teaching on MSc Financial Technology with Data Science, and will then switch to full-time PhD study for the remaining three years. Theo's research will explore privacy in distributed blockchain-based systems.
