A warm welcome to Dr Zixing Song, who has joined University of Bristol as Lecturer in Financial Technology. Zixing has a PhD in Computer Science and Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, on [trustworthy graph-based semi-supervised learning](https://repository.lib.cuhk.edu.hk/en/item/cuhk-3550523). He has also worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the [Machine Learning Group at the University of Cambridge](https://mlg.eng.cam.ac.uk/about.html), UK, where he explored the generative models for graph data. Zixing’s research interests include the trustworthy applications of Graph Neural Networks in finance.
