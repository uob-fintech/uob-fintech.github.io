I'm pleased to share that Prof John Cartlidge (PI) and Dr Jin Zheng (PI) have been awarded a **University of Bristol Global Positioning Fund (University Strategic Partners)** to establish a new research collaboration in Financial Technology with IIT Bombay co-investigators Prof S.V.D. Nageswara Rao and Dr Piyush Pandey. The GPF award will support the inaugural [University of Bristol–IIT Bombay FinTech Research Collaboration](https://uob-fintech.github.io/collaborations/iit-bombay/), 1–2 July 2026, Bristol, which will include a two-day programme of research presentations and strategic discussions. The collaboration aims to develop long-term partnerships in Financial Technology research, researcher mobility, and research-led education between the two institutions.
