I'm pleased to share that **Prof John Cartlidge** (PI) and **Dr Jin Zheng** (PI) have been awarded a **University of Bristol Faculty of Science and Engineering Global Positioning Fund (GPF)** award to establish a new research collaboration in Financial Technology with **IIT Bombay** co-investigators **Prof S.V.D. Nageswara Rao** and **Dr Piyush Pandey**. The GPF award will support the inaugural **[University of Bristol–IIT Bombay FinTech Research Collaboration](https://uob-fintech.github.io/collaborations/iit-bombay/)**, including a two-day programme of research presentations and strategic discussions to be held at the University of Bristol on **1–2 July 2026**. The collaboration aims to develop long-term partnerships in Financial Technology research, researcher mobility and research-led education between the two institutions.
