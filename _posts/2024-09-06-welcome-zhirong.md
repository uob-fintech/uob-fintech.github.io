A warm welcome to Ms Zhirong Zhang, who is visiting us for a 12-month PhD visit, supervised by Dr Jin Zheng. Zhirong is a third-year PhD student School of Information Engineering, [Chang'an University](https://en.chd.edu.cn/), China, under the supervision of Prof Yong Fang. Her visit to Bristol is funded by China Scholarship Council (CSC). While at Bristol, Zhirong will be exploring optimization and GNN applications in finance.
